How To Tell if a Source is Credible:


Ask yourself some of these questions to help determine if your information is reliable!


  • Can you find at least 2 other reputable sources that are publishing similar information?
  • Is the website known for being satire?
  • Check the date the article was published, if it's not too recent, try and find another source
  • Use fact checking resources like Snopes.com that help see if your source is reliable
